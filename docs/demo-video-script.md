# Demo Video Script

1. Introduce the project: "This is the AI Weather Forecast App, developed by Srinivas Gummadidala for the PM Accelerator AI Engineer Internship technical assessment."
2. Show the laptop-first dashboard, dark/light mode, and responsive navigation.
3. Search for a city and point out current weather, feels-like temperature, humidity, pressure, wind, visibility, sunrise, sunset, UV index, timezone, and description.
4. Scroll through the 5-day forecast and charts for temperature, humidity, and wind trends.
5. Open the Air Quality section and explain AQI and pollution details.
6. Show AI recommendations for clothing, travel, outdoor activity, and weather warnings.
7. Add a favorite location, search from favorites, and show history persistence through Supabase.
8. Export weather history as JSON and CSV.
9. Show Google Maps and YouTube travel videos for the searched city.
10. Demonstrate current location weather and voice search.
11. Show city comparison and mention PWA/offline shell caching.
12. Close with architecture: React/Vite frontend, Express backend, Supabase PostgreSQL, real weather APIs, modular services, validation, caching, and error handling.
